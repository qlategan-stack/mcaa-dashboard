## Notes

- **Camera-flagged entries** (`BLG108 CAMERA`, `PLOT 100 NEL CAMERAS`) trigger Rule 3: their payments route to the Camera Payment column instead of the EFT column. The member identification is the same as the non-camera variant.
- **Multi-ref members** (where column C has multiple comma-separated tokens) are matchable via any of their tokens. For example, Nazam (row 396) responds to `BLG108`, `BLG108 CAMERA`, and `EFT REF 108 BLG`.
- **Plot numbers with suffixes** (`8a`, `148/1`, `249/1`, `54-1`, `12/1`, `73/1`) must be matched as full strings to disambiguate from the bare number.
- **Multi-plot members** (Gerald Bezuidenhout owns plots `1,3,7`) count as ONE member for resident counts, but THREE plots for the unique-plot count (per the data model rule: split commas, exclude `?` and blank).
